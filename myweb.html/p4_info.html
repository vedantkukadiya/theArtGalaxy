Learn the beautiful art of modern calligraphy hand lettering – and be on your way to lettering everything from art pieces to cards and more.

In this beginner-level class, your instructors will begin by walking you through the basics of modern calligraphy while guiding you as you practice writing using worksheets. 
The instructors help as you begin developing your own personal lettering style, and finally, you will be able to create your own hand-lettered art piece or greeting cards.

In this Workshop You Will be able to –
Learn the basic elements of modern calligraphy and practice writing the alphabet
Begin to develop your own lettering style
Practice writing words and phrases
Make finished piece(s) including an art print and/or gift cards
